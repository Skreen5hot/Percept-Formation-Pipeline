Percept-Formation-Pipeline

A percept-formation data pipeline: raw input refined into structural then semantic types, fallibly bound, then deterministically adjudicated.

Three points on the honesty ladder. Dirty: a driver column of mixed string/numeric ids — bound and met, yet flagged uncertain (usable, correct, shaky). Mislabeled: two vehicle columns and no driver — the adjudicator rejects the forced agent mapping and names the law.
