Binder — Semantic Binder (SFB)Gate

Gate — the synthesis band (Binder + OCE) waits on one shared artifact: the compiled constitutive law (W2Fuel: ontology + RCR). The Binder fallibly proposes that the rows fall under a conceptual frame and assigns each column a role in it — those frames live in that law, so without it there is nothing to bind against. Data stops here.

OCE — Ontological Constraint EngineGate

Not reached — same gate as the Binder. OCE deterministically adjudicates a concept's constitutive necessity against the bound percept, and that necessity is the compiled constitutive law (W2Fuel: ontology + RCR) — the very artifact the Binder needs for its frames. The synthesis band gates on it as a pair, not as two independent stages.
